Veg Rice

Description

Vegetable Fried Rice is a popular and versatile dish enjoyed across various Asian cuisines,
known for its delightful combination of fluffy rice, colorful vegetables, and savory seasonings.
The dish is often cooked on high heat to achieve a slightly caramelized texture, enhancing the flavors of the individual ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Vegetables
  • onions
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • chopped cabbage
  • chopped green beans
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until translucent.
    Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir-fry for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the cooked rice.
  5. Drizzle soy sauce evenly over the rice and vegetables.
  6. Stir-fry everything together, mixing well to ensure the rice and vegetables are evenly coated with sauce.
    Adjust seasoning with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.
