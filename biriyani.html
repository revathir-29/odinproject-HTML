Biriyani

Description

Biryani is a classic dish originating from the Indian subcontinent, featuring fragrant rice layered with marinated meat or vegetables, infused with aromatic spices like turmeric,
cumin, and garam masala.
Garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and sometimes hard-boiled eggs, Biryani is known for its rich flavors and enticing aroma, making it a beloved culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • Ghee or Oil
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Green chilli
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Coriander and mint leaves
  • Chilly powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Curd
  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat
  2. Add Garam masala, Onion, Green chilli, Ginger garlic paste to the pan.
  3. Saute until the onions turn transparent
  4. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, tomato and curd
  5. Add masala,salt and chicken. Saute for a few more minutes, stirring regularly.
  6. Pour in 8 cups of water
  7. Add the rice.
  8. Serve. Once the rice has cooked, serve the biryani hot.
