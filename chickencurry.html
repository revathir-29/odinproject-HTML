Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken Curry is a beloved dish found in various culinary traditions worldwide, known for its rich,
aromatic flavors and tender chicken pieces simmered in a fragrant sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Ginger carlic paste
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Garam Masala
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Turmeric Powder

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with turmeric powder,cumin powder,coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste.
    Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat 3-4 tablespoons of oil or ghee in a large pot or deep skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add large finely chopped onions, Ginger carlic paste and saute until golden brown.
  4. Add finely chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pot. Stir well to coat the chicken with the masala.
    Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is lightly browned.
  6. Add salt to taste and 1 teaspoon garam masala. Stir to combine.
    Cover the pot and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice, naan, or roti.
